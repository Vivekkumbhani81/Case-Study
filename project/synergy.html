About The Project

The Synergy Online Clinic initiative aimed to transform healthcare accessibility by integrating innovative digital solutions into the traditional medical setup. The project centered around three core pillars: online appointments, digital waiting rooms, and instant teleconsultation. The primary goal was to bridge the gap between patients and healthcare providers, offering convenience, efficiency, and immediate access to medical assistance.

At its core, the project was designed to redefine patient-doctor interactions and streamline medical processes by focusing on three fundamental components: online appointment scheduling, digital waiting rooms, and instant teleconsultation services.Synergy Health 360 has multiple locations for your in-person appointments and affordable telehealth appointments with subscription models that can cover same-day consultations or unlimited monthly visits for your family.
