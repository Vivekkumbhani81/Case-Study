About the project

The Viyom Online Grocery Android app is a cutting-edge solution designed to revolutionize the way users shop for groceries. With a sleek and intuitive interface, this app provides users with a convenient platform to browse through a wide range of grocery products, place orders, and schedule deliveries seamlessly from their Android devices. Developed with a focus on user experience, Viyom offers features such as personalized recommendations, flexible payment options, and real-time order tracking, ensuring a hassle-free and enjoyable shopping experience. By leveraging the power of technology, Viyom aims to simplify grocery shopping, saving users time and effort while providing access to high-quality products at their fingertips.

Boasting a user-centric design, Viyom offers intuitive features such as intelligent search functionality, personalized recommendations, and secure payment options, ensuring a frictionless shopping experience. With a commitment to quality and reliability, Viyom leverages advanced technologies to streamline operations, optimize inventory management, and facilitate timely deliveries. By empowering users with the flexibility to shop anytime, anywhere, Viyom endeavors to redefine the grocery shopping landscape, making everyday essentials readily accessible at the touch of a button.

Technologies : TypeScript, React with Redux, Nest.js, Kotlin.
